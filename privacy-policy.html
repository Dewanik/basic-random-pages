Privacy Policy for GraphCluster

Last updated: April 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GraphCluster handles your information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

1. Data Collection and Use

GraphCluster is designed to operate offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information, notes, mind maps, or data to any external servers. All data created within the application remains on your device and under your control.

2. Local Data Persistence

All data, including your nodes, scenes, and cluster settings, resides exclusively on your device. We have no access to this data, and it is not backed up or synced to any cloud services by us. If you delete the application, your data will be deleted as well, unless you have backed it up independently.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs. We do not partner with external companies to monitor your activity or display advertisements within the application.

4. Payments

GraphCluster requires a one-time payment of $4.99 to access the application. This payment is processed exclusively through the official application store (Apple App Store or Google Play Store) using their secure payment systems. We do not handle your payment information directly.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not target or knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from children under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
